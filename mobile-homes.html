Mobile Homes

A mobile home, also known as a house trailer, park home, or trailer, is a prefabricated structure built in a factory on a chassis, designed to be transported to a site and used as a permanent or temporary dwelling. While they can be moved, they are often left in one place permanently or semi-permanently.
