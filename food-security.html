Food Security

Food Security is a situation that exists when all people, at all times, have all the necessary means to access sufficient food to meet their needs and maintain a healthy life.

The Biggest Threats to Global Food Security are:

  1. Climate Change and Environmental Shocks,
  2. Population Increase and the Modern Food System, and
  3. Disruptions in the Food Chain
