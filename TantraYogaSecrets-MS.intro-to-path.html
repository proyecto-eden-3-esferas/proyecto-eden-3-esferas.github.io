Tantra Yoga Secrets: Introduction to the Path

Sattvic

When pranic, emotional, or mental energy is allowed its full and natural expression, it becomes neutral or sattvic. In this neutral state, all events of life are experienced as arising from a spiritual source. This fundamental shift-from an idea about omni­presence to an actual embodied awareness of the omnipresent nature of the Divine­, brings you home to your Self.
