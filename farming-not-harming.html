Farming, not Harming!

Modern farming is responsible for one third of carbon emissions, extensive soil loss, pollutions of ground water, deforestation, loss of biodiversity...

It is claimed that just by making our farming more efficient we can lessen its harmful effects drastically.

Despite its inputs and side effects, modern farming is grieviously failing to feed the world.
